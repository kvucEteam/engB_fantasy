Task: Harry Potter

Introduction

Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone is the first book in the British author J.K Rowling’s “series of children’s books, about the boy – Harry Potter – a quite ordinary boy, who turns out to be a great wizard.

At the beginning of the book we learn that he is living with his aunt, uncle and cousin, who all tend to treat him rather badly, but all of a sudden he is violently thrown into a world where there are witches, wizards, sorcerers, goblins, ghosts, spiders, giant snakes and much, much more, and we follow right behind him.

Harry is the hero everyone dreams of reading about. Brave, yet fearful, with a huge background that amazes us all. We enter into the story just before he learns that he is special. Enjoy.
